About Me

Hi there! I'm a game developer with a total of 4 years of experience, specializing in casual and hypercasual games. But I'm not limited to just that - I've also worked on AR, VR, and metaverse projects, and enjoy taking on new challenges.

With a focus on mobile games, I've honed my skills in creating bite-sized fun through small experiences, but I also love big-scale games with complex mechanics and deep stories.

Currently, I'm looking for my next adventure in game development - if you have a quest for me, don't hesitate to reach out at cebiozgur@outlook.com or through the links below.
